This repository provides Magma implementations accompanying the paper Cryptanalysis of Definite and Indefinite Lattice Isomorphism Problems. The codes solves decision and search LIP of DEFI, and support the experimental results on attacks against DEFI, with parameter-specific instances and data files for reproducibility. It is intended for research and verification purposes, enabling readers to replicate and further explore the computational aspects of the cryptanalytic techniques presented in the paper.
